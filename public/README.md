# North Forge AI Studio

### AI-Powered Video Production for Pine Barron Farms

---

North Forge is a custom creative tool that helps the team at **Pine Barron Farms** turn real farm moments into cinematic video prompts — optimized for today's leading AI video platforms.

From cave hunts in the Pine Barrens, to dirt track racing, to the quiet magic of the Big Red Barn at golden hour — North Forge transforms those stories into production-ready prompts with the right cast, the right location, and the right platform in mind.

---

## Meet North

**North** is the AI creative director who lives in the loft of the Big Red Barn.

He knows every member of the crew. He knows every corner of the farm. And he knows exactly how to frame a shot for Sora 2, Kling AI, VEO 3, or Grok Aurora.

North doesn't do generic. Every prompt he writes is anchored to real people and real places — because that specificity is what makes great content.

---

## What You Can Do

- **Forge a Scene** — describe what you want to capture, and North writes a full production prompt: cast, location, camera direction, mood, platform, and duration
- **Browse the Cast** — every crew member is locked to their AI character ID for consistent results across sessions
- **Explore Platforms** — compare Sora 2, Kling, VEO 3, and Grok Aurora side-by-side to pick the right tool for the shot
- **Chat with North** — free-form conversation with your AI director, anytime
- **Rock Lab, Racing, Weird NJ** — dedicated rooms for Pine Barron Farms' signature content categories
- **North Digest** — weekly content calendar of every scene you've forged, grouped by week with viral scores
- **My Profile** — define how you appear in Sora 2 videos: age range, build, hair, eyes, style, up to 3 Sora IDs, and preferred duration
- **Sora Scout** — ask North for the latest Sora 2 tips, techniques, and best practices on demand
